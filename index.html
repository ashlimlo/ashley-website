About Me

Hello! I'm Ashley, a MSc Data Science and Machine Learning student at the National University of Singapore. I recently graduated with a degree in Data Science and Analytics, and I'm passionate about applying data-driven approaches to solve real-world challenges.

Currently interning as a Big Data Engineer at Tencent and a recipient of the IMDA Postgraduate Scholarship. My experience spans data engineering, machine learning, NLP, and generative AI across companies like Temasek, ShopBack, Shopee, and foodpanda.
